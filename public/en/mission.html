Mission

The mission of this website is to collect information about victims of the Iranian regime's violence against the people of Iran, to amplify the voice of the people in Iran outside of Iran, and to work for the freedom of Iran.

Who Runs This Website

This website is run by a group of volunteers dedicated to the freedom of Iran. The website is not connected to the Iranian regime in any way.

Security Commitment

All entries are securely stored. The website follows the strictest security standards to protect data and preserve the integrity of the website.
